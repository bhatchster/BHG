The Band

About

Barefoot Hockey Goalie is a San Diego-based original rock band whose music blurs the line between the confessional and the cinematic. Drawing on post-punk grit, classic songwriting craft, and an instinct for melody that lodges itself somewhere behind your sternum, BHG makes songs that feel like they've always existed.

The band's debut album Clairemont is named for the San Diego neighborhood where much of it was written — a place defined less by landmarks than by long flat streets, afternoon light, and the particular loneliness of being twenty-something and surrounded by people. The record captures that feeling with unflinching honesty and more than a few great hooks.

"Music that sounds like a city at 2am — restless, warm, and not quite ready to go home."

Formed around the songwriting partnership of Toe Knee Padderelli and Blair Hatch, BHG has spent the last several years building a devoted local following through relentless gigging, word of mouth, and the kind of live show that makes people feel less alone. Clairemont is the document of that journey — and the starting pistol for whatever comes next.
